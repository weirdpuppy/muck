about the comic

muck is a comic written and illustrated by roxanne coburn. it started in february 2018.

it follows the life of jojo, a twenty-one year old who lives in the fictional city of krasniva, ussa.


about the artist

roxanne coburn is a young person from a small town in kentucky in the southern united states. she first began working on MUCK while still she was a student at university.

this is her first attempt at a full-blown webcomic, but she has literally been fantasizing about this day since she first started following online comics as a much smaller human back when dial up was still a thing.

roxanne currently resides in carmel, indiana with her favorite human, tucker and her tiny hellion cat, jinx

for more of roxanne’s work you can visit her main website here.
